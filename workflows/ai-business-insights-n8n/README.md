# 🚀 AI Business Insights & Alert System (n8n + AI)

An AI-powered automation workflow that monitors business performance daily and sends insights directly to your inbox.

---

## 🎯 Overview

This workflow uses **n8n + AI agents** to:

* 📊 Analyze business data from Google Sheets
* 🚨 Detect revenue drops
* ⚠️ Identify low lead situations
* 📩 Send automated email alerts and reports

No dashboards required — everything is delivered via email.

---

## ⚙️ Workflow Architecture

Google Sheets → Data Processing → AI Analysis → Decision Logic → Email Alerts

---

## 🔥 Features

* ✅ AI-generated business insights
* ✅ Revenue drop detection (>30%)
* ✅ Low leads detection (<30)
* ✅ Priority-based alert system
* ✅ Daily automated reports

---

## 🧠 Use Cases

* Small business monitoring
* Startup analytics
* Sales & marketing tracking
* Automated daily reporting

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* n8n (workflow automation)
* OpenAI (AI analysis)
* Google Sheets (data source)
* Gmail (email delivery)

---


## ⚡ Setup Instructions

1. Import the workflow into n8n
2. Connect:

   * Google Sheets account
   * OpenAI API
   * Gmail account
3. Update your Google Sheet with:

   * Date, Sales, Leads, Revenue
4. Run workflow manually or schedule with Cron
